A Reckoning (2020, Installation, Four channel video total run times 10:49 and 14:48)

A demonstration of three battles with hierarchy. The first shows three videos of a burning castle, over layed with a subject attempting to block a stream with his body. The second is a pedestal of brick, where a fantasy card game is interrupted by a more animal man. The last is a broken window.
