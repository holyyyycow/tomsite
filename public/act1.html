Phase Change: Act I (2019, installation, video run time 25:02)

The first of a three part year long installation. A stream simulator features a small town decaying as water runs through the sculpture and the show proceeds. It presents erosion, a macro process, which existed long before and will exist long after us, in a micro, observable setting. Demonstrating an attempt at control, it reveals powerlessness. Complementing the interactive sculpture is a documentary exploring cultural, social, and political relationships within the hydrosphere.
