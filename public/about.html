I am a multidisciplinary conceptual artist and organizer living and working in Baltimore, maryland.

I work with organic matter and found objects over extended periods of time as I explore ideological and subconscious structures and conflicts.

I’ve created site-specific installations in galleries and artist run spaces in Baltimore, Frederick, Washington, DC, as well as on public water in the Potomac River 

I perform in a noise duo called Contacts, generating and remixing live exponential conference calls.

Currently, I am the technical director and a programmer of quarantv, a volunteer public-access-style streaming channel, which ran 24/7 with no repeat programs for the first 1800 hours of the Corona virus pandemic and features original video work, talk shows, musical performances, youtube compilations, and local news. Now streaming once a week.
